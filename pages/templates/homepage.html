Welcome to Jabe Investment

JABE INVESTMENT is a trusted leader in the supply of heavy-duty and automotive machinery spare parts. With over 15 years of experience, we have established ourselves as a reliable partner for industries that depend on machinery, ranging from construction and mining to agriculture.

Our commitment to quality and service excellence has made us a preferred choice for businesses seeking reliable machinery solutions and spare parts.

Access a wide range of heavy earth moving machinery – excavators, bulldozers, wheel loaders, dump trucks, graders, and more – ready to tackle the toughest jobs. We source from top-tier manufacturers to ensure every machine delivers durable, reliable performance.

Keep your fleet running at peak efficiency with our extensive selection of high quality spare parts and specialized lubricants. Using authentic parts guarantees a perfect fit and consistent performance, preventing the risks that come with unreliable knock-offs.
