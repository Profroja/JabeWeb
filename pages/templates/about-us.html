About Us

Welcome to Jabe Investment

JABE INVESTMENT is a leading professional manufacturer and supplier of mining machinery spare parts, specializing in high-quality components for heavy mining equipment operations. Our product range includes bucket pins & bushings, track chains, rollers, bolts and nuts, and other precision components.

Located in the heart of the industrial sector, we provide comprehensive solutions for all your construction and machinery needs. Our team of experts ensures that every project is completed with precision and efficiency.

"Quality & Service" is our core principle. We pride ourselves on delivering exceptional service, competitive pricing, and reliable support. Whether you're looking for heavy machinery, spare parts, or technical consultation, we're here to help your business grow.
